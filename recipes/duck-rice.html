A delicious duck rice plate

A duck rice made with ducks raised on a farm and the rice specially selected from the best rice field on earth.

Ingredients (per 2 persons):

  • Duck - 500g
  • Rice - 300g
  • BLack pepper - 10g
  • Salt - 30g
  • Carrots - 2units
  • Tomato - 3units
  • Water - 700ml

Steps:

  1. Put the duck with black pepper, carrots and tomato on a pan
  2. Turn on the fire and stir it up
  3. Add the water and salt
  4. As soon as the water has a brown color add the rice and turn off the fire
  5. Leave the pan closed by 20 minutes
  6. LEt's eat!!
